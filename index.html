    Turntable.fm + Last.fm = Love

    tt.fm scrobbler updates your last.fm records for you when you're chillin out to tunes on turntable.fm. - and it's so easy to use, even grandma can keep an archive of her filthy dubstep tracks!

    Instructions:

    1. Drag this link: tt.fm scrobbler to your bookmarks bar*
    2. Click the bookmarklet everytime you enter a turntable.fm chatroom
    3. Follow the last.fm login instructions and... BOOM! scrobbles everywhere

    *tt.fm scrobbler is only supported on Firefox at the moment.
